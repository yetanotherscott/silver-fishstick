HOW TO USE MAP LINKS

Clicking a "View on Map" link will:

  1. Open the Overworld map in a new tab
  2. Center the map on the coordinates
  3. Place a red dot at the exact location
  4. Zoom in to show the area clearly

Note: The red dot is temporary and will disappear if you move the map. To return to the location, click the link again.
